Shopping Cart

The shopping cart shows you what products you have purchased, and the quantity in which you purchased them. You can select an item in the list to get more information about it, and if you decide that you don't want to purchase a specific product anymore, you can click \"Remove From Cart\" while it is selected. When you are ready to make a purchase, you can enter your city and address, then click \"Purchase\" to purchase the items that are currently in your cart.
